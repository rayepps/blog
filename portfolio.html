The Village Website @ villagesite.com

While working at Aumnia, I worked closely with the marketing team at Village in Santa Barbara to both design and develop a new site that represented their brand. They wanted a site that said elegance and class as well as a site that had the power to handle and manage thousands of market listings and users synchronously. We implemented a killer fast ecosystem using a .Net API, a .Net Core App, Entity Framework, and Automapper.

Entrokey @ entrokey.com

I built the Entrokey (entropy + key) page in part so that people could use it to quickly and easily generate unique passwords but mainly to demo the entrokey.js library I created. Its a small and simple way to generate highly random and highly unique keys. Check it out on GitHub
